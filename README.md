## About

Machine learning scientist and educator with 15+ years across academic
research, industry advisory, and post-secondary teaching at scale. Hands-on
depth in PyTorch-based deep learning, with a reinforcement-learning
pedigree from the University of Alberta.

- **Currently:** Instructor at [NorQuest College](https://www.norquest.ca/),
  teaching in the
  [Machine Learning Analyst Diploma (MLAD)](https://www.norquest.ca/programs-and-courses/programs/machine-learning-analyst/) —
  an ASTech Award–winning program I helped design as principal technical
  designer, built under the federally funded
  [AI Pathways Partnership](https://www.amii.ca/about/ai-pathways-partnership).
- **Previously:** ML Scientist & Educator at
  [Amii](https://www.amii.ca/) — designed and delivered the flagship
  [Machine Learning Technician](https://www.amii.ca/courses) program
  (8 cohorts during my tenure, average NPS ≈ 70) and led the hands-on
  technical content for Amii's Coursera specialization
  [*Machine Learning: Algorithms in the Real World*](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/machine-learning-algorithms-real-world).
- **Earlier:** Senior Teaching Assistant & Research Assistant at the
  University of Alberta. Helped Prof. Richard S. Sutton teach 8
  iterations of the graduate Reinforcement Learning course. Doctoral
  research on best-arm identification in structured bandit problems.
- **Research interest:** deep learning, generative AI, and the
  intersection of generative AI with reinforcement learning.

## Showcase

[**cmpt2500w26-project-tutorial**](https://github.com/NorQuest-MLAD-Courses/cmpt2500w26-project-tutorial)
— a production-grade, end-to-end MLOps project I built as the tutorial
foundation for my CMPT 2500 *Machine Learning Deployment & Software
Development* course. Covers the full lifecycle:

- **Data pipeline:** pandas, NumPy, DVC.
- **Training & tuning:** scikit-learn, MLflow experiment tracking.
- **Serving:** Flask REST API with Swagger (Flasgger), Gunicorn.
- **Containerization & deployment:** Docker, Docker Compose, Google
  Cloud Run.
- **CI/CD:** GitHub Actions (test on PR; build, push, deploy on merge to
  main).
- **Monitoring:** Prometheus + Grafana for infrastructure and training,
  Evidently for data-drift detection, Prometheus alert rules.
- **Testing:** 17 pytest tests covering preprocessing, training, config,
  and API.
